Frequently Asked Questions

Mindset Analyzer uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze your daily activities, mood patterns, and lifestyle factors. It collects data through various sources and provides personalized insights and recommendations to help you maintain a positive mindset.

We collect data about your mood, energy levels, sleep patterns, physical activity, and other lifestyle factors. All data is collected with your consent and stored securely.

For best results, we recommend updating your data daily. You can set up automatic reminders in the settings to help you maintain a consistent schedule.

Insights are generated using machine learning models that analyze patterns in your data. The system considers multiple factors and provides personalized recommendations based on your unique situation.
